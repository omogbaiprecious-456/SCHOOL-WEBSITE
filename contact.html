Frequently Asked Questions

❓ When do admissions open?

Admissions typically open in December for the following academic year. Please contact our admission office for specific dates.

❓ What are the admission requirements?

Requirements include academic transcripts, entrance examination, and an interview. Details available on our admission page.

❓ Do you offer scholarships?

Yes, we offer merit-based and need-based scholarships. Contact our financial aid office for more information.

❓ What are the school hours?

Regular school hours are 8:00 AM to 3:30 PM, Monday through Friday. Before and after-school programs available.
